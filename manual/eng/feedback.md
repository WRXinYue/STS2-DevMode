# Mod feedback

Open from the in-run rail or title screen **DEVMODE → Diagnostics → Mod Feedback**.

- Title + description, export **ZIP report** for mod authors
- Optional game log tail attachment; **privacy mode** redacts user-data paths
- ZIP includes: mod list, filtered logs, Harmony dump, framework snapshot, combat stats JSON, and your report text
- Saved under `user://devmode-reports/`
